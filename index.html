Trivia:

  • Megumin has her own spin-off series along with Yunyun, titled Kono Subarashii Sekai ni Bakuen wo!
  • In the anime's PV character introduction, Megumin was introduced as the "Real Heroine", while Aqua was introduced as the "Main Heroine".
  • Her clansmen all have weird names, which sound like childish nicknames in Japanese. "Megumi" is a rather popular girly name in Japan, and the suffix "in" is used to "girlify" or diminute a name. So, "Megumin" is an overboard pun on a common name, thus the humor.
  • Megumin parodies Lina Inverse of Slayers series. Some of their similarities include:
    • Both use the strongest spell. However, Megumin's trump spell is her only spell.
    • Both hate being called loli or their unfavorable, but very fitting, nicknames.
    • Both have self-proclaimed rival, but Naga is extremely haughty, while Yunyun is extremely shy.
    • Both have an even more amazing sister: elder sister Luna and younger sister Komekko.
    • Both give off the same feel, possibly due to their similar appearance.
  • In anime, Megumin's full Explosion spell chant is very similar to Lina's Dragon Slave chant. However, Megumin tends to shorten her chants, or add praises about herself or Crimson Magic Clan. Also, it's probably a good idea that Megumin doesn't know any spell similar to Giga Slave.
  • Megumin's appearance may also partially parodied I-No of Guilty Gear games, particularly the gloves and the smiling witch hat. However, it is confirmed that Megumin will likely remain petite, despite her wish to have sexy body after she grows up.
  • She has a "cat" named Chomusuke which is actually the other half of Lady Wolbach. She wears lacy black panties (depicted as white in Light Novel).
  • She claims that she doesn't need to use the washroom and insists on such even when Kazuma accompanies her to use the toilet in their party's mansion.
  • She hates dungeons because using Explosion would collapse them and kill everyone inside.
  • Megumin refuses to learn any other spells despite Kazuma's insistence.
  • All of Megumin's equipment (staff, eye-patch, and outfits) are gifts from her school friends.
  • In the anime, Megumin upgraded her staff twice. Her original staff had blue orb. In Episode 4, it's changed into crimson orb. In Episode 10 ending scenes, she added 3 colored crystals to her staff.
  • Megumin sends her extra money home. She also writes about their adventures, which are vastly different retelling, where she played grand and central roles.
